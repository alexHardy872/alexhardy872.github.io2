My Story

I originally went to college for Exercise Science to become a personal trainer or strength coach. after graduating, earning my certifications and working for a while I realized that it wasn't really what I wanted to do with my life.


I used to be very involved in coaching and competeing in the sport of Powerlifting. When I was, fitness and lifting was my passion. So it always seemed like I was working towards doing what i WANTED to do. But that enthusiasm did not last long once I left the school environment.

I became distracted by endevors I was more passionate about and became dissatisfied with the diminishing intellectuall returns of trying to stay financially afloat in the fitness industry.


I left the field and started working factory jobs. After 2 years working nights in a fermentation plant I finally decided to make a move for a new career.

I have always enjoyed creative endeavors from movie making to painting , writing, and many other things. I've also always done well in school and I generally like learning new things.

A friend convinced me that a good career path for me might be to be a software developer. After deciding on computer programming as my new direction I am starting devCode Camo with zero prior coding background so let's hope it goes O.K.!


My main hobby these days is Music. I play bass in Mud Dog and Versio Curs.
